Classes & Workshops

Learn, Create, Connect

From beginner drawing to advanced oil painting, our classes and workshops offer something for every skill level and age. Members receive discounted tuition on all programs.

Spring & Summer 2026

✎ Class Image
Drawing Beginner

Drawing & Sketching Fundamentals

Thursdays, April 17 – May 22, 2026 6:00 – 8:00 PM  ·  6 Sessions

Build a strong foundation in observational drawing, line quality, shading, and composition. All materials included. No prior experience needed.

$85 Members  /  $105 Public
🎨 Class Image
Painting All Levels

Watercolor Explorations

Saturdays, April 19 – May 17, 2026 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM  ·  5 Sessions

Explore wet-on-wet techniques, color mixing, and loose expressive washes. Beginners and returning students both welcome. Materials list provided.

$95 Members  /  $115 Public
🎨 Class Image
Painting Intermediate

Oil Painting Workshop

Fridays, May 2 – June 6, 2026 1:00 – 4:00 PM  ·  6 Sessions

Cover color theory, underpainting, and glazing techniques. Students should have basic drawing skills. Canvas and brushes provided; oil paints extra.

$125 Members  /  $150 Public
🏛 Class Image
Ceramics All Levels

Introduction to Hand-Building

Wednesdays, April 16 – May 21, 2026 6:30 – 8:30 PM  ·  6 Sessions

Learn pinch, coil, and slab construction methods to create functional and sculptural forms. Clay and two firings included in tuition.

$110 Members  /  $130 Public
📷 Class Image
Photography Beginner

Photography for Beginners

Saturdays, May 10 – May 31, 2026 1:00 – 3:30 PM  ·  4 Sessions

Understand exposure, composition, and light using your own camera or smartphone. Final session includes a short gallery walk and critique.

$65 Members  /  $80 Public

Mixed Media All Levels

Collage & Mixed Media

Tuesdays, April 22 – May 27, 2026 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM  ·  6 Sessions

Combine paper, paint, fabric, and found objects to create layered, expressive works. Each session introduces a new technique or material.

$85 Members  /  $100 Public

Art History All Levels

Lecture: American Impressionism

Thursday, May 7, 2026 6:00 – 7:30 PM  ·  Single Session

A curated lecture exploring the American Impressionist movement with a focus on works held in Midwestern collections. Q&A to follow.

Free Members  /  $5 Public

Youth Ages 8 – 14

Youth Summer Art Camp

July 7 – 11, 2026 (Mon – Fri) 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM  ·  5-Day Intensive

A week of drawing, painting, printmaking, and sculpture for young artists. Each camper takes home a portfolio of finished work. Snacks included.
