Alice Baber: From the Permanent Collection

January 24, 2026 – May 31, 2026

Weil Gallery

Alice Baber (1928–1982) was a pioneering American abstract painter celebrated for her luminous, color-saturated canvases that seem to glow from within. This exhibit draws from the museum's permanent collection to showcase the breadth of her lyrical, light-filled work.
