Your Gift Keeps Art
Alive in Lafayette

For over 115 years, the Art Museum of Greater Lafayette has been a free, community-supported institution. Every dollar you give directly funds exhibitions, education programs, and open doors for everyone.

Our Mission

Why It Matters

Every gift directly funds what happens inside these walls.

Exhibitions

Your support brings rotating exhibitions — from regional artists to nationally recognized collections — to our galleries, free of charge to every visitor.

Education & Youth Programs

From youth art camps to adult workshops and school outreach, gifts fund the classes and programs that put art in the hands of the next generation.

Free & Open to All

We believe art belongs to everyone. Donor support is what makes it possible to keep our doors open — and our admission free — every day we are open.
