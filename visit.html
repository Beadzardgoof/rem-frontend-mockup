Admission

Always Free

Admission to the Art Museum of Greater Lafayette is free every day we are open — for everyone. No tickets, no reservations needed for general admission.

Donations are always welcome and help keep our doors open and our programs running. Make a gift →

While You're Here

Make the Most of Your Visit

Gift Shop
Browse locally-made prints, jewelry, books, and art supplies in our on-site gift shop.

Docent Tours
Guided tours are available for groups. Contact us in advance to schedule a tour tailored to your interests or age group.

Classes & Workshops
We offer art classes for all ages — from watercolor and pottery to youth programs. View classes →

Accessibility

ADA Parking

Accessible parking spaces are located on 9th Street adjacent to the museum entrance.

Accommodations

The museum strives to provide reasonable accommodations for all visitors. Please contact us in advance if you require specific assistance.

Contact Us

For accessibility requests, reach us at 765-742-1128 or visit in person during museum hours.

Groups & Schools

Bring Your Group to the Museum

We welcome school classes, scout troops, senior groups, community organizations, and businesses. Tours can be tailored to specific interests and include hands-on activities in our studio classrooms.
